Help & About

Release:

About DEFCON Parties Calendar

The longest-running unofficial party list for Black Hat, BSides Las Vegas, and DEF CON in Las Vegas. This beta is a searchable front-end for the live party feed — built for planning your Hacker Summer Camp week, tracking what you have applied to, and spotting new listings as vendors publish them.

All times are shown in Pacific Time (PT). Event signup links open the vendor's registration page; we do not run registrations on this site.

What's New

  • New since your last visit: Parties added since you last closed the calendar get a New badge.
  • Updated since your last visit: Existing parties with changed title, time, location, or description get an Updated badge (amber).
  • Party reminders: In an event's details, choose 15 min, 30 min, or 1 hour before start, or Off to cancel. Personal alerts only — not a site-wide subscriber push. On localhost you can allow system notifications; on a LAN IP (e.g. 192.168.x.x) reminders alert while this tab stays open.
  • Mark all seen: Clears New and Updated badges and updates your baseline when you have caught up.
  • Smarter refresh during HSC: Auto-sync every 6 hours Aug 1–11 (every 48 hours off-season). Refresh Now forces a sync (5-minute cooldown).
  • Show New / Updated filter: In Card or Agenda view, focus on unseen or freshly synced parties only.

Viewing Events

  • Card View: A visual grid of all events. Click a card for details and signup.
  • Agenda View: Events grouped by day with time ranges. Good for building a day-by-day plan.
  • New / Updated badges: Shown on cards, agenda rows, and the event modal when a party is new to you, newly added on the last sync, or edited since your last visit.

Personalizing Your Schedule

  • Approved (Green): Mark events you plan to attend. These count toward your Party-O-Meter™.
  • Pending (Blue): Mark events you have applied to but have not heard back on yet.
  • Rejected (Red): Mark events you were turned down from so they stay out of your way.
  • Your choices are saved automatically in this browser — no account required.

Filtering & Finding Parties

  • Search Bar: Search by title, location, date, or description. Paste a URL to submit a missing party.
  • Show New / Updated: Show only events with a New or Updated badge.
  • Show My Approved / Pending / Friends: Focus on your schedule or imported friend schedules.
  • Agenda day filters: In Agenda View, filter by weekday (Mon–Sun).
  • Hide Past / Rejected: Trim noise from events that already happened or you ruled out.
  • Keyword Filters: Permanently hide events matching words you choose (e.g. breakfast, brunch) under Share/Sync → Keyword Filters.

Staying Up to Date

  • Last refreshed: Shown above the event list with the current auto-refresh interval.
  • New / updated counts: Above the list — e.g. 2 new · 1 updated since your last visit. Use Mark all seen when you are caught up.
  • Refresh Now: Pulls the latest calendar from the server. Use after you hear about new parties or before planning your day.
  • Leaving the site: When you close or hide this tab, we save a snapshot of event IDs (and edit fingerprints) so the next visit can highlight what changed.
  • First visit: Existing parties are not marked New — only additions after your baseline appear.
  • Subscriber push alerts (optional, site-wide): Not the same as per-party reminders. When enabled, the DCP team can send a manual or scheduled broadcast from the staging pipeline — you would opt in with a Subscribe to party alerts button (coming soon on this page). Calendar edits alone do not ping subscribers.

Sharing & Collaboration

  • Export My Schedule: Copy a code with your Approved / Rejected / Pending choices.
  • Import a Schedule:
    • Paste your own code to sync across devices.
    • Add a nickname to import a friend's approved list (shown in orange).
  • Manage Friends: Remove imported friend schedules from the Share/Sync panel.
  • Flex on 'em: Share your approved party count on X or email from the Party-O-Meter area.

Other Features

  • Party-O-Meter™: Progress bar for how full your approved schedule is.
  • Submit a Party: Use the Submit tab or paste a URL in search when an event is missing.
  • Cut & Paste: Store registration fields (name, email, title, shirt size, etc.) for fast form fill on Lu.ma, Google Forms, HubSpot, and similar signups.
  • Dark Mode: Toggle with the sun/moon button in the header.
  • Analytics: Sign Up, Approved, and Rejected clicks are tracked anonymously to see which events are popular.
  • Privacy: Schedules, filters, profiles, and "seen" baselines live in your browser only. Share codes are encoded, not encrypted — share only with people you trust.
