Help & Features

Viewing Events

  • Card View: A visual grid of all events.
  • Agenda View: A list of events grouped by day.

Personalizing Your Schedule

  • Approved (Green): Mark events you plan to attend. These are counted in your "Party-O-Meter".
  • Pending (Blue): Mark events you've applied to but haven't heard back from yet.
  • Rejected (Red): Mark events you were rejected from to keep track.
  • Your choices are saved automatically in your browser!

Filtering

  • Search Bar: Instantly search by title, location, date, or description.
  • Agenda Filters: When in Agenda View, you can filter by day of the week and hide events based on their status (Past, Rejected, Pending, etc.).
  • Keyword Filters: Permanently hide events containing specific words (like "breakfast") using the "Keyword Filters" section.

Sharing & Collaboration

  • Export My Schedule: Generates a code that saves all your event statuses (Approved, Rejected, Pending).
  • Import a Schedule:
    • Paste a code without a nickname to sync your own schedule across devices.
    • Paste a code with a nickname to import a friend's approved schedule, which will appear in orange.
  • Manage Friends: Easily remove imported friend schedules.

Other Features

  • Party-O-Meter™: A fun progress bar that shows how full your approved schedule is.
  • Flex on 'em: Share your party count on X or via email with one click.
  • Submit a Party: Use the "Submit a Party" tab or search for a URL to suggest new events for the calendar.
  • Cut & Paste: Save your registration info for yourself and friends to easily copy/paste into forms.
  • Smart Refresh: Calendar data is cached for 48 hours to reduce load times. You can force a refresh every 5 minutes with the "Refresh Now" button.
  • Analytics: Clicks on "Sign Up," "Approved," and "Rejected" are tracked anonymously to help us understand which events are the most popular.
